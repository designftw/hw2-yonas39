This section will evaluate and discuss the strength and weaknesses of the Graphic Design of MIT Management Sloan School Office of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion website
As per The Principle of Design there are multiple ways of analyzing a graphics design, i.e,.

  1. Alignment
  2. Repetition
  3. Proximity
  4. Contrast
  5. Simplicity

For the sake of this assignment, I will discuss about the alignment and repetition aspects of the MIT Management Sloan School Office of Diversity,Equity and Inclusion webpage.

Alignment

The MIT Management Sloan School Office of Diversity,Equity and Inclusion webpage did well in organization of the website. In its home page the name of the office was clearly written in a white color aligned to the center of the page with a beautiful colorfull background image. Right under the page title there is a small box that contain 4 directory elements that also is aligned to the center. Following the directory elements box there are texts, images and list of people. All texts are aligned to the center except the one's that needs some emphasis. As of Feb 20, 2023, the website given an emphais to a box of text by breaking the alignment the webpage follows. In adition, they used all the same method when they introduce an image to the website. either they put the image on the left and write discription to the right of an image or they put the image and description followed under the image. it make it easy to follow along for the users and give a good structure for the website.
