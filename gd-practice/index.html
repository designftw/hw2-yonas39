In order to design the Business card, letter head, and envelop I followe the following design principle we learned in class.

  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Contrast
  • Simplicity
Repetition:
I used the company logo, name and color theme at different places and it helps users to easily identify wifyme products and familiarize users with the color theme of the compnay, brand logo.
Alignment:
I used alignment widely during business card production. becasue the given space was limited and we need to place compnay logo, employee name, title adress and website alignment is a great tool to place each of this important items in the correct place. It helps users to focus on the information they want.
Contrast:
Although the color was given, encorporating the color of the logo with the background color and theme is crucial for readablity and Simplicity of the design.
Simplicity:
In general, a bussiness card, letter head, envelop or any other design should be simple for the users to access the information we want to pass along. So that I kept my designs simple and straight forward for the users.
